1. Company Overview

Datopic Technologies Private Limited is an India-based emerging technology and innovation-driven IT services company, incorporated on 7 February 2017 under the Companies Act, 2013. Its Corporate Identification Number (CIN) is U74999DL2017PTC312445, and it is registered with the Registrar of Companies, Delhi.

The company’s registered office is located at RZ-77A, Dabri Extension, Main Palam Road, New Delhi – 110045, India. Operationally, Datopic has also established a significant presence in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, serving as its primary technology and development hub.

Datopic Technologies is guided by the vision of “transforming data into intelligence and innovation into action.” Its central focus lies in building next-generation digital solutions for enterprises, leveraging cutting-edge technologies such as Artificial Intelligence (AI), Blockchain, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, and Data Analytics.

With a lean yet talented workforce (estimated 20–50 professionals), Datopic has rapidly built credibility through specialized digital transformation projects, catering to both domestic and international clients. The company aims to empower organizations to reimagine their operations, enhance decision-making, and achieve sustainable digital growth through the intelligent application of emerging technologies.

Key leadership includes Mr. Saurabh Jain and Mr. Sumit Chauhan, both experienced technology professionals with deep expertise in enterprise systems, digital solutions, and IT consulting.

3. Services & Technology Portfolio

Datopic’s expertise covers a broad spectrum of digital transformation services, strategically grouped under five main service lines:

a) Product Engineering and Software Development

Datopic specializes in end-to-end product lifecycle management, helping clients conceptualize, design, develop, test, and deploy software applications and digital platforms. The company focuses on agile and DevOps methodologies to ensure rapid delivery with high scalability and maintainability.

Technologies used include Python, Java, Node.js, React, TensorFlow, AWS, and Azure, among others. Solutions range from enterprise web applications to AI-integrated products and IoT dashboards.

b) Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics

Datopic’s data division focuses on predictive analytics, natural language processing (NLP), and computer vision. It helps organizations harness raw data to gain actionable insights through machine learning models, data visualization, and intelligent automation.

Typical use cases include:

Predictive maintenance for manufacturing.

Fraud detection and anomaly identification for financial systems.

AI chatbots and recommendation systems for customer engagement.

c) Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Management

In today’s data-driven economy, Datopic offers robust cybersecurity and network protection solutions, including penetration testing, risk assessment, endpoint security, and identity management.

Its cybersecurity frameworks follow NIST and ISO 27001 standards, providing clients with compliance-ready and secure IT infrastructure.

d) Blockchain and Digital Transformation

Datopic has been an early adopter of blockchain, working on supply chain transparency, digital identity, and smart contract implementation. The company provides blockchain consulting, DApp development, and system integration with traditional architectures to create trustless, decentralized ecosystems.

e) Strategic Consulting and IT Modernization

The company provides strategic guidance to organizations aiming to modernize legacy systems. Their consultants assess current workflows, recommend cloud migration strategies, and design digital roadmaps aligned with business goals.
